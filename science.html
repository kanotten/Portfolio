Science Center

This project is a website for a science center themed around the universe and space, collaboratively developed by Kenneth Sheikh and Patrick Røethe. The site comprises 4-6 main pages, featuring an engaging animation on the start page and a checkout section. Key features include a start page animation of Earth orbiting the sun, responsive design through media queries, a user-friendly dropdown menu, and seamless button navigation linking to respective pages. The primary technologies used are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Experiences

Developing this science center website has been an immensely enriching experience in web development. Working on this project, I gained hands-on experience with several key aspects of modern web design, including creating responsive layouts, implementing engaging animations, and developing intuitive navigation systems. Collaborating with Patrick Røethe, each of us took responsibility for at least two pages, ensuring a cohesive and comprehensive final product. The project also involved making thoughtful design decisions aimed at creating a kid-friendly and educational platform. While the current version of the website meets our initial goals, we recognize areas for improvement and plan to make further enhancements post-delivery. This experience has significantly broadened my skills, particularly in collaborative development and dynamic design, and I continue to revisit and refine the project to keep my competencies up to date.
